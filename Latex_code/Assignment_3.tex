\question: 16 : Events E and F are such that P(Not E or not F)=0.25, State whether E and F are mutually exclusive.\\
\solution :\\
As given in the question ;\\
$$\pr{\bar E+\bar F}=0.25$$
As , $\pr{\bar E+\bar F}=\pr{\overline{(\bar E \bar F)}}$\\
$$\implies \pr{\overline{\bar E \bar F}}$$
$$\implies 1-\pr{EF}=0.25$$
$$\implies \pr{EF}=0.75$$

$$\because \pr{EF}\neq 0$$
$\therefore$ E and F are not mutually exclusive events.
